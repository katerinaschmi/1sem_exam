BASIC RULES

First, place the mat face-up on a flat surface. Have each player remove his or her shoes and place them to the side (out of the way). Or, use the shoes to anchor the corners of the mat to keep it smooth and straight. Choose one person to be the referee. This person is not a player. He or she is responsible for spinning the spinner, calling out the moves and monitoring the game. Position yourself correctly on the map according to the number of players, be it 2, 3 or 4 people.

After whirling the spinner, the referee calls out the body part and color where the arrow points to. Then, accordingly, players must place, on the mat, that body part on that one color. If a knee or an elbow lands on the mat, or if any player falls over, they're out! The last player left in the game wins.

But most importantly, be creative and HAVE FUN!
